个人简介About Me

我是香港中文大学物理系的博士研究生，主要研究方向为量子物理和凝聚态物理。我对理论物理和计算物理都有浓厚的兴趣， 特别是在量子多体系统、相变理论以及数值计算方法方面。

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Physics at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. My research focuses on quantum physics and condensed matter physics. I have strong interests in both theoretical and computational physics, particularly in quantum many-body systems, phase transition theory, and numerical methods.

研究方向Research

量子多体系统Quantum Many-Body Systems

研究强关联量子系统的基态性质和激发态行为，包括量子相变、纠缠特性等。 使用数值重整化群(NRG)、密度矩阵重整化群(DMRG)等方法进行计算研究。

Investigating ground state properties and excited state behavior of strongly correlated quantum systems, including quantum phase transitions and entanglement properties. Utilizing numerical methods such as Numerical Renormalization Group (NRG) and Density Matrix Renormalization Group (DMRG).

相变理论Phase Transition Theory

探索量子相变和经典相变的普适性质，研究不同维度下的临界行为。 特别关注拓扑相变和对称性破缺相变的理论描述。

Exploring universal properties of quantum and classical phase transitions, studying critical behavior in different dimensions. Special focus on theoretical descriptions of topological phase transitions and symmetry-breaking phase transitions.

计算物理方法Computational Physics Methods

开发和应用高效的数值算法求解复杂的量子多体问题。 包括蒙特卡洛方法、张量网络方法以及机器学习在物理中的应用。

Developing and applying efficient numerical algorithms for solving complex quantum many-body problems. Including Monte Carlo methods, tensor network methods, and applications of machine learning in physics.
